## ✨ Introduction

**Mage MOVIE VISPER MD** is a powerful WhatsApp Multi-Device (MD) bot built on the **Baileys** library[reference:3][reference:4]. It is designed to search, stream, and download movies seamlessly, turning your WhatsApp into an ultimate entertainment hub[reference:5]. With a robust plugin system[reference:6] and extensive command library[reference:7], it automates tasks and enhances your messaging experience[reference:8].

---

## ⚡ Features

- 🎬 **Movie Magic** – Search, stream, and download movies directly from WhatsApp[reference:9].
- 🤖 **AI-Powered** – Smart replies and AI chat integration[reference:10].
- 🛠️ **Modular & Customizable** – Easy-to-edit command system[reference:11] and plugin support[reference:12].
- ⚙️ **Group Management** – Kick, add, promote, demote, tagall, welcome, and more[reference:13].
- 📥 **Downloader** – Supports YouTube, Spotify, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and more[reference:14].
- 🎨 **Media Conversion** – Sticker maker, image converter, TTS, URL shortener, and more[reference:15].
- 🔒 **Security & Anti-Spam** – Anti-link, anti-delete, anti-spam, and auto-react features[reference:16].
- ⏱️ **24/7 Stability** – Optimized for continuous uptime and fast response[reference:17].
- 🧩 **260+ Commands** – Extensive library for all your needs[reference:18].
